Who We Are

About Patel Security

We started ShieldCore because small and mid-sized businesses deserve the same level of protection as large enterprises - without the complexity or cost.

Our Story

Built for Businesses Like Yours

Patel Security was founded in 2018 by a team of network engineers and former IT security analysts who kept seeing the same problem: big cybersecurity firms only served big companies. Everyone else was left to figure it out alone.

We built ShieldCore to change that. Today we protect businesses ranging from 1 employee to over 5,000, offering the same standard of protection at every level.

Our Mission

"To make enterprise-level cybersecurity accessible, understandable, and affordable for every business."

Security should not be complicated or out of reach. Our team turns technical complexity into clear, manageable protection. We are not here to sell you tools you do not need - we are here to protect what you have built.
